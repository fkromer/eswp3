Patterns

This project is solution-oriented. Therefor no **antipatterns** or **"smells"**
can be found explicitly. References to them are stated implicitly in the
explicit meta-data **Problem** of specifc patterns instead.

About the meta-data
===================

All patterns do have meta-data in common: the **name**. It is very important for
remembering and for communicating the ideas of the patterns. It should be as
short as possible but without neglecting expressivness and uniqueness.
